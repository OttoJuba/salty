Hi There! I'm Otto Juba

Ever since I was a child I have felt like an engineer. Starting with finding ways to terrify my mother by building mini-bike jumps in the woods or fabricating tree "forts", I slowly grew into more sustainable pursuits.

I originally migrated towards mechanical engineering for its plethora of end uses and studied towards that end while at the Unversity of Cincinnati. While living in Cincinnati I was fortunate enough to be introduced to the local hackerspace, Hive13 by a friend and started frequenting the 2600 meetings held there.

Once my eyes were opened to the power of coding I could never turn back. I decided to pivot my degree and pursue Computer Science at Cleveland State University and I have never looked back.

Over the years I have picked up many hobbies ranging from diving to soldering together my own drones.
